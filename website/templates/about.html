about us

soccer zeal

Based in Goa, Soccer Zeal Sports Club is a professional football club, that currently competes in the Goa Professional League. Founded in 2023, the club has gone on to become one, if not the most successful club, Indian Football has ever witnessed, currently under the ownership and guidance of Mr. Shrinivas Vasudeva Sinai Mapusa, Chairman of the Zeal Group of Companies, the club has begun to invest huge time and money in the development of talent, right from the Grassroots level all the way to the senior team.

Faith in Youth

The Golden Eagle model of development, stresses on the Vision of the Club & the belief that the way forward is to develop a system of progression whereby each player is given the best chance to progress forward. From our club Grassroots Program all the way through to our Senior team, Youth Development is always at the forefront of the picture as we continue to put onus and have that commitment to their development, repaid in the progress we continue to see. In 2020 alone, Dempo Sports Club were crowned Champions in the GFA U-14 & GFA U-16 tournaments as well as qualifying for the Hero Sub-Junior League, Hero Junior League & Hero Elite League tournaments, topping their zonal groups across all competitions & age groups.

  • Leland Lagos

    President

    Having inherited more than just the passion, concerns, and commitment from the past bosses, when it comes to matters of the team. He also inherited a sense of vision, something that helped him greatly, when he took over management of the club in its lean phase.

    Open-minded, balanced, exuding sincerity and transparency, he has inspired the joie de vivre so emblematic of the club’s on-field endeavours, an attitude ensured by building esprit de corps that binds players and officials alike.

    BORN 25 May 1975, Goa

  • Kenton Kidd

    General Secretary

    A relative of the Dempo family, the ever popular Vilas Sardesai had the license to call the shots at the club with whom he was associated with since the early seventies.

    A former President of the Goa Football Association and Vice-president of the All India Football Federation, Sardesai was a ‘doer’ of the first order and was associated with several successful campaigns both on and off the field. A larger than life personality, he truly left his mark on the club.

    BORN 4 January 1977, Goa
